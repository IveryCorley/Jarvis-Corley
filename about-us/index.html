About Me

My name is Jarvis Ivery-Corley. I’m a shy, kindhearted introvert born in Augusta, GA, and raised in the small town of Thomson, GA. I’m the oldest of two my brother and me. I’ve always wanted to be success-ful and accomplish so many goals. One day I spoke with God and asked him to lead me in the right direction and after that I started to write my own memoir and come up with hair care products. I have more in store because God isn’t done with me yet.
